I NYOMAN SUKARI
1968 - 2010
Bali, Indonesia
Artist
A mysterious man full of riddles, Sukari was an enigma.
An artist who seemed restricted with tradition & fierce, but also friendly and was an active leader of the art community; a man who lived a bohemian lifestyle, full of raw-talent and dynamicism, but also full of vision, principle, and philosophy.
Tengkorak Truyan (1995)
In all the contradiction that is the life of Sukari, he remains undisputedly a legend of Balinese art today.
